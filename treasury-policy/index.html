IPNS.io Treasury & Revenue Policy

Effective Date: February 18, 2026 · Version: 1.0

1. Scope

This Policy governs management and use of protocol revenue from IPNS.io registry operations and related gateway services.

2. Treasury Control

  • Treasury assets are controlled by a multi-signature Safe on Base.
  • Treasury actions require configured signer threshold approvals.
  • This Policy does not create custodial obligations to registrants.

3. Revenue

  • Name registration fees
  • Name renewal fees
  • Future protocol-related fees (if introduced)

4. Allocation Targets

  • 50% Founder/Builder Compensation
  • 30% Runway & Operations Reserve
  • 20% Ecosystem Grants / Bounties / Integrations

These are target allocations and may vary based on operational and legal requirements.

5. Compensation

Founder compensation is treated as a legitimate operating expense to sustain delivery, support, and security work.

6. Risk Controls

  • Prioritize continuity of core protocol operations.
  • Maintain reasonable reserve buffers.
  • Use conservative treasury assumptions under market volatility.

7. Transparency

IPNS.io intends to publish periodic treasury updates with high-level inflow/outflow categories and material policy changes.

8. Open Infrastructure

IPNS.io may publish open specifications and open-source components while retaining rights in operated services, trademarks, and domain branding.

9. Amendments

This Policy may be modified by authorized treasury governance. The published current version is controlling from its effective date.

Informational only. Not legal, tax, or investment advice.
